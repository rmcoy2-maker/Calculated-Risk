About Calculated Risk

We are not a sportsbook — we are strictly an AI-driven data analytics company. Born from a bored room, not a boardroom, Calculated Risk is built for fans and sharps who want sharper insights, cleaner data, and tools that turn odds into opportunities.

Our founder is a data scientist who understands both the thrill and frustration of navigating probabilities. That experience drives us to design models that put the power in the user’s hands. Unlike “the house,” we aim to even the playing field — empowering you to calculate, challenge, and reshape risk.

Mission

To deliver defensible analytics that help our community make more informed, responsible, and confident decisions.

Vision

To build the most trusted, user-driven sports analytics platform — one that continuously evolves through community feedback, modeling innovations, and transparency.

Our Approach

  • User-First: Built for consumers, not corporations.
  • Agnostic About Gambling: We focus on providing data, not opinions.
  • Sharpening the Edge: With backtesting, modeling, and unrivaled features, we help reduce the gap between “just staying in the game” (53% hit rate) and winning consistently.
  • Community-Driven: Our models grow stronger as users interact, share feedback, and stress-test insights.

We don’t just beat odds. We rearrange them in your favor.

Calculated Risk provides analytics for informational and entertainment purposes only — we are not a sportsbook.
